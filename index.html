How to Use This Generator

  • Fill in all the required information in the form on the left

Compliance Guidelines

  • All healthcare staff must wear their ID badges while on duty
  • Badges must clearly display the employee's name, designation, and specialty
  • Photos should be professional and recent (within the last 6 months)
  • Badges should be properly signed by both the employee and issuing authority
  • Valid dates must be clearly displayed and regularly updated
  • Lost or damaged badges should be reported immediately
